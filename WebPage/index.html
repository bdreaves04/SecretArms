Our Mission Statement

Shopping online can be a pain. Finding the best deals, using coupon codes, getting cashback, remembering passwords, collecting points, organising delivery... and then doing it again and again, for every single website. If you find this annoying, then Allshop is the service for you. We are an integrated shopping experience that takes the hassle out of online shopping. All shop aggregates listings from online retailers in one searchable database. We collate all delivery restrictions, taxes and fees, returns policies and other relevant information and display it to you in a uniform and consistent way. When it comes to paying, we handle all of the payment details automatically using our Allshop Wallet service. If that wasn't enough, Allshop Service goes even further. Allshop can analyse your data to predict your shopping patterns and make your online experience even better. Our targeted suggestions might just help you remember that things you forgot you wanted. If shops sign up for the AllShop Vendor service, we can arrange personalised adverts and special offers to be displayed to you without revealing your personal habits to the vendor in question, so your data remains secure. They wont know if you've seen a special offer unless you use it. Allshop House can keep track of what food, household products and other consumables you have in your household and automatically restock things as you need them.
